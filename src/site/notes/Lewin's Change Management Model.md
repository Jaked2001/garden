# [[Lewin's Change Management Model\|Lewin's Change Management Model]]




Change process is in 3 stages:
1. [[#Unfreeze]] people
2. [[#Change]]

## Unfreeze

Ensure the employees are ready for change.


### Pre HRC training

HRC: Human Robot Collaboration


## Change

Execute the intended change.

Collaborative Process training: about technical and operational aspects. What is needed to operate a robot.
## Refreeze

Ensure that the change becomes permanent.
