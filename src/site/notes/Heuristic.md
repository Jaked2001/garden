# [[Heuristic\|Heuristic]]

```ad-Definizione
title: Heuristic

A **heuristic** or **heuristic technique** is any approach to [problem solving](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Problem_solving "Problem solving") that employs a [pragmatic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pragmatism "Pragmatism") method that is not fully [optimized](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mathematical_optimisation "Mathematical optimisation"), perfected, or [rationalized](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rationality "Rationality"), but is nevertheless "good enough" as an [approximation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Approximation "Approximation") or [attribute substitution](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Attribute_substitution "Attribute substitution").

```
